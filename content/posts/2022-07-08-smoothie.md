# Ingredients
* 1 banana
* 1 apple (Braeburn)
* 1 orange
* 12 fresh dates
* 12 tablespoons of tahini
* 200g of greek yoghurt
* dash of milk
* juice of half a lemon
* 200g frozen blueberries
* a pinch of salt
